PARPI Footnotes
  • 1/ Real personal income for metropolitan and nonmetropolitan state portions is personal income divided by the RPPs and the national PCE price index. The result is a constant dollar estimate of real personal income, in millions of constant 2012 dollars. Statistics presented in thousands of dollars do not indicate more precision than statistics presented in millions of dollars.
  • 2/ Real per capita personal income for metropolitan and nonmetropolitan state portions is calculated using Census Bureau midyear population estimates based on the 2010 Census. Estimates for 2010-2020 reflect county population estimates available as of March 2021. BEA will incorporate midyear population estimates based on the 2020 census results when they become available.
  •  Last updated: December 14, 2021-- new statistics for 2020; revised statistics for 2008-2019.
Source: Department of Commerce / Bureau of Economic Analysis / Regional Economic Accounts
